Why Rusty

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Single binary

12 MB static binary. No runtime, no dependencies, no container. Just download and run.

Local-first privacy

Zero telemetry. Your code never touches our servers. You choose where your requests go.

Sandboxed execution

Commands run in a sandbox. Rusty asks before editing, deleting, or executing anything risky.

Bring your own model

Works with OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, and any OpenAI-compatible endpoint. Your choice.

Terminal-native

Lives where you work. No context-switching to a browser or IDE. Just type and go.

Open source

AGPL-3.0 licensed. Read every line, audit every claim. Build it yourself if you want.
